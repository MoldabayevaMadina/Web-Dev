Recipe #1: Delicious Avocado Sandwich

  • Time: 10min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Provolone cheese 2 slices
Avocado 1/4
Tomato 1/2
Balsamic vinegar 1 1/2 teaspoons

Step 1: Lay 1 slice bread on a plate. Place provolone cheese on bread and top with avocado and tomato slices, respectively. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over tomato and top with remaining slice of bread.
